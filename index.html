About the Artist

Full Name: Maria Chandria Mariel Ollage Leu
Stage Name: Yielziey
Birthday: April 19, 2003 (Age 22)
Birthplace: La Union, Philippines

Chandria Mariel, professionally known as Yielziey, is a rising Filipino-Swiss singer, songwriter, gamer, and programmer. Hailing from Caloocan City, she is known for her heartfelt lyrics and soulful melodies that blend emotional storytelling with modern pop sounds.

Her music, available on all major streaming platforms, explores themes of love, hope, and self-discovery, resonating deeply with her listeners.
