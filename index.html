About the Artist

Chandria Mariel Ollage, professionally known as Yielziey, is a rising Filipino singer, songwriter, gamer, and programmer. Hailing from Caloocan City, she is known for her heartfelt lyrics and soulful melodies that blend emotional storytelling with modern pop sounds.

Her music, available on all major streaming platforms, explores themes of love, hope, and self-discovery, resonating deeply with her listeners.
